Frequently Asked Questions

How do I translate a word?

Type a word in the search field, choose the source and target languages, then tap the Translate button.

How do I save a word?

After translating a word, tap the Save button. Saved words appear in the “My Words” section.

Where are my saved words stored?

Saved words are stored locally on your device. They are not synced to a server.

Why can’t I save more words today?

Free users have a daily save limit. You can wait until the next day or upgrade to Pro for unlimited saves.

I purchased Pro but it’s not active

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Make sure you are signed in with the same App Store account used to purchase
  • Use “Restore Purchases” if available

Text-to-speech is not working

  • Check your device volume and silent mode
  • Make sure the selected language voice is available on your device
